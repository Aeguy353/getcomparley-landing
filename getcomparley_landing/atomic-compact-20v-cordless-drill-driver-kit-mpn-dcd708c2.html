Product Details

ATOMIC Compact Cordless 20 V Li-ion Brushless Mid-Handle Drill/Driver Kit (DCD708C2) by DeWALT. The Brushless Compact Drill/Driver Kit includes two 20V MAX Lithium Ion batteries, charger, and carrying bag.
