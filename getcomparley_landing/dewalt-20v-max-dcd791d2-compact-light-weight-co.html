Details & Specs

20V MAX* XR® Li-ion Brushless Compact Drill / Driver Kit - Includes DCD791 - 20V MAX* XR® Li-Ion BL Compact Drill / Driver, (2) DCB203 - XR® 2.0AH Battery Packs, Charger, Kit Box, Belt Hook
The DCD791D2 20V MAX* XR® Li-Ion Brushless Compact Drill / Driver Kit is lightweight and compact for working in tight spaces for long periods of time. High-speed transmission delivers 2 speed variations allowing users to choose the level of performance needed for various applications. Comfort grip adds balance and control.
