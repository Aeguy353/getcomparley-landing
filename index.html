Related Brands Under DEWALT’s Parent Company

DEWALT is part of Stanley Black & Decker, a global tool manufacturer that also owns several well-known brands. While DEWALT tools are not rebranded under other names, these related brands share similar designs, manufacturing roots, or target different users:

  • BLACK+DECKER – Affordable, light-duty tools for homeowners.
  • Craftsman – Mid-range DIY tools, some powered by similar 20V platforms.
  • Porter-Cable – Phasing out, but formerly offered budget pro-grade tools.
  • Stanley – Known for hand tools, not power drills.

These brands cater to various users — from casual DIYers to pros — but DEWALT remains their premium performance line.
